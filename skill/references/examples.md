# Example Prompts for `gpt-image-2`

Adapt these directly when the user asks for prompts.

## 1. Premium product ad

```text
Create a photorealistic product advertisement for a premium coffee grinder.
Scene/background: warm minimalist kitchen with travertine counter and soft morning light.
Main subject: matte black coffee grinder centered in the scene.
Key details: brushed metal accents, subtle reflections, clean countertop, premium packaging nearby.
Composition: eye-level shot, product occupies about 35% of the frame, negative space on the left for headline text.
Lighting/mood: soft natural daylight, polished and premium.
Style: photorealistic, high-end editorial commercial photography.
Constraints: no watermark, no extra text, no clutter, no exaggerated props.
```

Revision prompts:
- `Keep everything the same, make the lighting warmer and more luxurious.`
- `Change only the background to a darker premium kitchen at dusk.`
- `Preserve the product exactly; make reflections more realistic and subtle.`

## 2. Poster with strong typography

```text
Create a modern promotional poster for an AI workshop.
Layout: portrait poster with strong top-to-bottom hierarchy.
Main visual: sleek abstract image-generation artwork in the center.
Text to include: "PROMPT LIKE A PRO" and "Master AI image prompting in one session".
Typography: bold uppercase white sans-serif for the headline, smaller clean sans-serif for the subtitle.
Text placement: headline at the top center, subtitle directly below it.
Color system: charcoal black background with electric blue accents.
Style: premium, sharp, modern tech event branding.
Constraints: no extra text, perfectly legible typography, high contrast, no watermark.
```

Revision prompts:
- `Keep the layout, make the visual more elegant and less noisy.`
- `Make the poster feel more premium and less startup-generic.`
- `Preserve the text and hierarchy, improve spacing and legibility.`

## 3. Ecommerce cutout / catalog image

```text
Create a clean ecommerce catalog image of a luxury skincare bottle.
Scene/background: pure white seamless studio background.
Main subject: one frosted glass skincare bottle with a matte cream cap.
Key details: premium label, soft reflection under the bottle, realistic glass texture.
Composition: centered product shot, front-facing, lots of clean whitespace.
Lighting/mood: soft studio lighting with crisp edges and subtle shadow.
Style: photorealistic, premium beauty catalog photography.
Constraints: no watermark, no extra objects, no extra text, no messy reflections.
```

## 4. Surgical image edit

```text
Change only the background to a bright premium bathroom with neutral stone textures and soft window light.
Keep the skincare bottle shape, label, camera angle, proportions, and product color exactly the same.
Do not alter the text on the label, the cap finish, or the bottle reflections more than necessary.
```
