# Products

Use this reference when the user wants product photos, catalog shots, ecommerce images, packaging visuals, beauty shots, or product edits.

## What matters most

Prioritize:
- product accuracy
- materials and texture realism
- clean composition
- believable reflections and shadows
- packaging/text preservation when editing
- consistent angle across variants

## Prompt structure for product work

```text
Create a [catalog shot / beauty shot / hero product image] for [product].
Scene/background: [studio / kitchen / bathroom / desk / lifestyle setting]
Main subject: [product]
Key details: [material, color, packaging, label, texture]
Composition: [front-facing, three-quarter angle, centered, top-down]
Lighting/mood: [soft studio, morning light, luxury glow, crisp retail]
Style: [photorealistic, premium ecommerce, editorial product photography]
Constraints: no watermark, no extra props, no packaging drift, no label changes
```

## Good language for product shots

Use phrases like:
- `photorealistic`
- `premium ecommerce photography`
- `clean seamless background`
- `realistic glass texture`
- `subtle reflection`
- `accurate label details`
- `luxury packaging`

## Core product modes

### 1. White-background catalog image

```text
Create a clean ecommerce catalog image of a luxury skincare bottle.
Scene/background: pure white seamless studio background.
Main subject: one frosted glass skincare bottle with a matte cream cap.
Key details: premium label, subtle reflection, realistic glass texture, accurate proportions.
Composition: centered product shot, front-facing, lots of clean whitespace.
Lighting/mood: soft studio lighting with crisp edges and gentle shadow.
Style: photorealistic premium beauty catalog photography.
Constraints: no watermark, no extra objects, no extra text, no messy reflections.
```

### 2. Premium lifestyle product shot

```text
Create a photorealistic hero image for a premium espresso machine.
Scene/background: warm modern kitchen with travertine counter and refined styling.
Main subject: brushed metal espresso machine in active use.
Key details: polished steel, realistic steam, ceramic cup, accurate reflections.
Composition: three-quarter angle, machine is dominant but not cropped, balanced counter styling.
Lighting/mood: soft morning light, luxurious and inviting.
Style: editorial product advertising photography.
Constraints: no clutter, no extra text, no watermark, no distorted proportions.
```

### 3. Surgical product edit

```text
Change only the background to a premium neutral bathroom with soft natural window light.
Keep the bottle shape, label, cap finish, camera angle, proportions, and product color exactly the same.
Do not alter the text on the label or introduce any additional props.
```

## Revision prompts for product work

- `Keep everything the same, improve realism and material detail.`
- `Preserve the product exactly; make the reflections softer and more premium.`
- `Change only the background to a clean white studio.`
- `Keep the packaging identical, make the scene feel more luxurious.`
- `Use the same product, but create a more commercial ecommerce look.`

## Failure fixes

### Label changed
Add:
- `keep label exactly the same`
- `do not alter packaging text`
- `preserve product identity and packaging details`

### Looks fake
Add:
- material details
- better lighting language
- `photorealistic`
- realistic reflections / subtle shadows

### Too much clutter
Add:
- `only essential props`
- `clean surface`
- `minimal background`
- `product remains the clear focal point`
