# Ads

Use this reference when the user wants prompts for ads, campaign creatives, social ads, banners, hero sections, or promotional visuals.

## What matters most

Prioritize:
- clear commercial intent
- strong focal point
- room for copy / CTA
- premium mood control
- minimal clutter
- fast variant generation

## Prompt structure for ads

```text
Create a [ad type] for [product / service / campaign goal].
Audience: [who this is for]
Scene/background: [environment]
Main subject: [hero object/person]
Offer or message focus: [what must be felt or understood]
Composition: [placement, negative space, orientation]
Lighting/mood: [luxury, energetic, clean, urgent, playful]
Style: [photorealistic / editorial / bold poster / premium 3D]
Text to include: "[EXACT TEXT]" (if needed)
Constraints: no clutter, no extra text, no watermark, ad-ready composition
```

## Good language for ads

Use phrases like:
- `landing page hero image`
- `social media ad creative`
- `premium campaign visual`
- `clean ecommerce banner`
- `negative space for headline copy`
- `clear focal hierarchy`
- `scroll-stopping visual`

## Common ad patterns

### 1. Hero with copy space

```text
Create a wide landing page hero image for a premium AI platform.
Audience: startup teams and marketers.
Scene/background: elegant modern workspace with subtle depth and warm neutral tones.
Main subject: sleek interface and one confident focal object representing image generation.
Offer or message focus: modern, premium, powerful but approachable.
Composition: widescreen, main subject slightly right of center, generous negative space on the left for headline and CTA.
Lighting/mood: soft cinematic daylight, refined and premium.
Style: photorealistic product marketing visual.
Constraints: no watermark, no extra text, no clutter, no cheesy sci-fi elements.
```

### 2. Product + emotion ad

```text
Create a photorealistic social ad image for an artisanal coffee brand.
Audience: premium coffee lovers.
Scene/background: moody kitchen counter at sunrise.
Main subject: premium coffee bag and freshly brewed cup.
Offer or message focus: warmth, ritual, quality.
Composition: close-up hero shot with clean breathing room for copy in the upper-left corner.
Lighting/mood: warm cinematic light, indulgent and premium.
Style: editorial food advertising photography.
Constraints: no extra text, no clutter, no watermark.
```

## Revision prompts for ad work

- `Keep the layout, make it feel more premium and expensive.`
- `Preserve the subject, create more clean space for headline text.`
- `Make the ad more energetic and contrasty without adding clutter.`
- `Change only the mood from luxury to playful and youthful.`
- `Keep the composition, make it more conversion-focused and less artistic.`

## Failure fixes

### Feels generic
Add:
- target audience
- offer/message focus
- stronger mood words
- clearer composition and commercial use

### Too busy
Add:
- `minimal background`
- `only essential props`
- `clean hierarchy`
- `negative space for copy`

### Feels like art, not ad
Add:
- `advertising visual`
- `campaign creative`
- `brand-ready`
- `clear focal hierarchy`
- `commercial polish`
